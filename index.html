My testimony

I could not describe the joy I felt when I discovered these works of the post-apostolic followers of the Lord Jesus. I was ignorant and been thinking that the history of the faith would be about Roman Catholicism or adulterated things, to my astonishment it was anything save that. Praise God they were Catholic __Universally fellowshiped none of them claim to be anything save disciples who with trembling sought to be like their masters__ not Roman Catholic. I thank God for the privilege, to access these works of those who have been successors, nay co-workers with the very apostles of the Lord of Glory, discover their love for the Word of God even Christ, for all the saints as their apostles enjoins, their testimonies, their passion, their implemention of the commandments of the Lord, their dealings with the world, with the heathens, with the philosophers, with the heretics, ..., their matyrdoms, their love for each other, ... May the Lord Jesus grant renewal and His love to all believers everywhere!
