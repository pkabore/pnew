A testimony

Peace of Christ. My name is Paul, I am from West-Africa. I would like to encourage you to investigate the faith which has been handed down to us.

I cannot describe the joy I felt when I discovered these works of the post-apostolic followers of the Lord Jesus. I was ignorant and had been thinking that the history of the faith might be about Roman Catholicism or adulterated things. Yet, to my big astonishment, it was anything save that. Praise God they were Catholic __ geographically diverse yet doctrinally identic (Lyon, Carthage, Alexandria, Antioch, Rome, Smyrna, ...) rendering the same testimony to the Lord through matyrdom in the midst of tribulations and persecutions, none of them claimed to be anything save disciples who with much trembling sought to be like their Master __ but not Roman Catholic.

I thank God for the grace of being able to have in our possession these works of those who have been successors, nay co-workers with the very apostles of the Lord Jesus, so be able to discover their love for the Word of God even Christ, for all the saints as their apostles praised in the Scriptures, their testimonies, their passion, their implemention of the commandments of the Lord, their dealings with the world, with the heathens, with the philosophers, with the heretics, their matyrdoms, their love for each other, etc. May the Lord Jesus grant renewal and repentance to all believers everywhere!
